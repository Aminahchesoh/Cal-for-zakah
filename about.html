About Zakah

Zakah   is an important pillar of Islam and is the act of giving a portion of one's wealth to those in need. It is a form of purification and a means of helping the less fortunate in society. The calculation of Zakah depends on various factors such as the type of wealth and its value.

In this website, you can calculate your Zakah based on your selected wealth and its value. Simply choose the type of wealth and enter its value in the provided fields, then click the "Calculate Zakah" button.
