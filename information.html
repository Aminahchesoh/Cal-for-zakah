About Website

"Cal for Zakah"   is a web application designed to assist individuals in calculating their Zakah, an important pillar of Islam. Zakah is the act of giving a portion of one's wealth to those in need, and this app provides a convenient way to determine the amount of Zakah based on different types of wealth.

How to using the application
1. Select the type of wealth from the dropdown menu. The available options include cash, investments, gold, and silver.
2. Enter the value of the selected wealth in the provided input field. The app supports decimal values with two decimal places.
3. Click the "Calculate Zakah" button to perform the calculation. The app will then display the calculated Zakah amount in the result section. It's a quick and efficient way to determine the Zakah obligation based on your specific wealth.
